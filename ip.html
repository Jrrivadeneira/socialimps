Intellectual Property

Intellectual property is a very significant topic in the movie. It could easily be argued that all the peril in the movie stems from the fact that a rival corporation is trying to buy trade secrets and company assets from Nedry. Assuming Hammond could guarantee secrecy during the registration process, it’s reasonable to assume that many of that many technologies seen within the park are patented. Furthermore employees are almost certainly asked to sign NDAs and documents preventing them from even talking about the fact that they’re resurrecting dinosaurs, let alone selling trade secrets and company assets to competitors. Thus, Nedry’s actions represent an enormous violation of the related IP laws.
